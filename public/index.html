The Challenge

Crop insurance under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) faces critical challenges that
impact millions of Indian farmers. The current system struggles with manual verification delays,
assessment bias, and scalability issues across diverse agricultural regions.

Delayed Claims

Manual verification takes weeks, leaving farmers in financial distress

Lack of Transparency

Opaque processes erode trust between farmers, insurers, and government

Manual Bias

Human assessment introduces inconsistency and potential bias in damage evaluation

Limited Scalability

Current methods cannot handle millions of claims across diverse regions

CROPIC Initiative by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

The government launched the Crop Insurance using Remote sensing, Smartphone & Artificial Intelligence
(CROPIC) initiative to modernize crop insurance assessment.

Goal:

Enable real-time, geo-tagged crop image analysis using AI/ML to provide fast, unbiased, and transparent
damage assessment for insurance claims under PMFBY.
